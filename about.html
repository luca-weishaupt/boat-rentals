About Us

My name is Ben, and I fell in love with the outdoors almost five years ago when I picked up my greatest passion, fishing. I feel extremely blessed to be the owner of a Jon boat on New York’s Kensico reservoir. Over the last four years of fishing on the beautiful waters of Kensico, I have noticed that I am usually the only one out there, even though hundreds of boats are registered to be on the water. The truth is that most boats scattered around the reservoir stand idle for the majority of the time. I remember fishing from shore, dreaming about being out there, on the middle of the water. A boat allows for unlimited accessibility, no more snags or material loss, and most of all, beautiful views paired with more catches. Keeping all of this to myself felt wrong. Over the years, I’ve introduced freshwater fishing to dozens of friends and relatives. There is no greater feeling than seeing someone’s eyes brighten when taking in the divine beauty that Kensico offers. I strive to create these lasting experiences for people in my community. With my brother's help, we want to get our increasingly digitized society back in touch with nature.

Mission

We want to let our community experience the breathtaking serenity of New York’s most exclusive bodies of water while portraying the state’s natural beauty in an exciting and never before seen way.

Vision

Our vision is to reignite our communities’ passion and appreciation for nature in an effort to help preserve and nurture it, one booking at a time.
